  • Moving Planner

  • 4-6 Weeks Before Move:
    1. Notify your local post office about your move.
    2. Create a list of businesses, relatives, and friends that need to know you are moving.
    3. Start selling, donating or giving away any items you don’t plan to bring with you.
  • 2-3 Weeks Before Move:
    1. Get your family ready for the move with going away parties and final visits.
    2. Prepare all your household items for packing and transport.
    3. Plan a date to have your utilities disconnected at your old home.
    4. Have any drapes or rugs cleaned.
  • 1-2 Weeks Before Move:
    1. Bring pets to the veterinarian for any last-minute check-ups or treatments.
    2. Give away or dispose of household plants you aren’t taking with you.
    3. Clean out your space at any local facility where you keep items, such as the gym or bowling alley.
    4. Return library books and any other borrowed items.
  • 1 Day Before Move:
    1. Thoroughly check all parts of your home, including inside closets and under beds, to guarantee that nothing is left behind.
    2. Point out fragile items to movers so they know to take extra care.
    3. Unplug any electronic items so they have time to cool off before moving day.

  • Packing Tips

  • 1. Pack rarely used rooms first.
  • 2. Dust items as you pack them, so they are clean when you remove them.
  • 3. Use strong boxes and keep supplies handy at all times.
  • 4. Wrap small items in colored tissue paper to ensure they stand out and are protected.
  • 5. Fill boxes completely; if a box has extra space, add packing material to fill in the gaps.
  • 6. Before sealing boxes, shake them make sure they are packed full to avoid denting.
  • 7. Place pillows, clothing and other textiles in clear garbage bags before packing them in boxes.
  • 8. Pack bathroom toiletries in plastic bags.
  • 9. Place a layer of packing material at the top of each box to avoid accidentally cutting the belongings when you open the carton with a utility knife later.
